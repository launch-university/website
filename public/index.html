Do you have a service business or product idea? Tri-Cities Launch University will provide insight from local business experts, who will help you have a better understanding of how to turn your idea into a business. It's an accelerated learning course, meeting every Thursday evening for four weeks.

Schedule

Here's what your time investment will provide.

Session 1

Thursday - March 31st @ 7pm

The first session will focus on Customer Validation. How to take your idea out to the world, and how to get valuable feedback to validate the idea swimming in your head.

Session 2

Thursday - April 7th @ 7pm

The second session will guide you through determining the correct business model for your idea. Matching the right model with your idea is a strategic way to understand the process of making money.

Session 3

Thursday - April 14th @ 7pm

The third session gets you thinking about the financial and legal aspect of business. How to operate with business partners, fundraising and avoiding costly mistakes.

Session 4

Thursday - April 21st @ 7pm

The final session will focus on business marketing, branding and communication. Understanding the importance of putting a name and a feeling with your idea.
